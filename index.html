About Me

All Roads Lead to Leadership: A story that resonates

Interest: Something that has been at the top of my list of interest since junior year of college is travel. Traveling has fostered an unmentionable amount of growth in not only my personal life but also within my career. I had the opportunity to work in Jordan and experience an entirely different cultural work environment.

Outside of work opportunities I gotten the opportunity to travel to places such as France, Hong Kong, Thailand, and Dubai to name a few of my favorites. Some of my other interest include dancing of which I use as a way to unwind from weekly stress, I love reading and reading.

Background: With a degree in International Business, experience working abroad, and extensive management experience I am confident in my ability to transition into a Project Management role.

Having little to no experience in content writing I relied on my passion for writing and resilience to land a content marketing role abroad. It was in this position that I learned how to work in an environment that was culturally different than anything I have had the opportunity to take part in. It was in Jordan that I learned how to communicate through language barriers, set my own project managemnt schedules, and work with very high-end clients on very short deadlines.

From there I moved into retail management when I learned how to not only manage a team but actively forecast store trends in order to heighten consumer purchase. It was in this role that I developed the desire to work in tech.

My aspirations for working in tech turned into a Product Specialist role with Wix.com where I was able to expand my knowledge as well as advance rather rapidly. I went from Product specialist to Communications Lead for Wix video, and a team lead shortly after.

Leading off of my work experience I was motivated to take a Web Development course in order to navigate further into the world of tech. Within this course I learned SQL, javascript, MongoDB and React skills that I can now use within any new roll.

My career so far shows that I am not afraid to take risk and learn new skills, I am now looking for a environment where I can actively use a culmination of all the skill I have mastered thus far.
