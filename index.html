About Me

When asked to write about yourself, you always hear "hey, make it funny" well, what if I am not funny... Then what? I wouldn't know as funny is one of my best qualities.

Outside of being a self-proclaimed comic, I love sports, dogs, and visiting some of the finest bars and clubs throughout the Bay area. Catch me soaking in the rays and catching flights on any given weekend.

From smiling to giving back in the community I am passionate about everything I choose to do.
